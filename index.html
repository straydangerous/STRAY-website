Story about Stray

Stray is a 2022 adventure game developed by BlueTwelve Studio and published by Annapurna Interactive. The story follows a stray cat who falls into a walled city populated by robots, machines, and mutant bacteria, and sets out to return to the surface with the help of a drone companion, B-12.

Gameplay

Stray

The game is a third-person adventure game. It has elements of an open world , as well as an emphasis on atmosphere and art. The player, controlling a ginger cat, must solve puzzles in order to progress through the story, moving obstacles and moving across platforms. The protagonist is accompanied by a satellite drone named B-12 (B-12), who can help by translating the robots' language and storing items found around the world. One of the game's enemies is the Zurks , which will attack the player in ferocious swarms.

B-12 drone
Drone explains that he previously helped a certain scientist, but most of his memory was damaged and he needs time to recover. B-12 promises to help the hero return to the surface and escorts him further into the city.
Momo
With the help of the Companions, the cat and B-12 make it to the Midtown sector, where they meet another "outsider" Clementine, who plans to steal a nuclear battery to power a subway train leading to the surface.
Walled City 99
Realizing that humanity's legacy now belongs to the Companions and the cat, B-12 sacrifices himself to open the blast doors. After their opening, sunlight falls on the city, which burns the Zurks and deactivates the Guardians.
